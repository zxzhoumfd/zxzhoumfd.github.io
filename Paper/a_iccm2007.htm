Proceedings of the 4th International Congress of Chinese Mathematicians III, Higher Education Press, 405-416 (2008)

Darboux transformations for some two dimensional affine Toda equations

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract
\noindent The Lax pairs for the two dimensional A2n(2), Cl(1) and Dl+1(2) Toda equations have a reality symmetry, a cyclic symmetry and a unitary symmetry. The Darboux transformations for these systems are discussed. These Darboux transformations should be of high degree. Exact solutions are written down by computing the Darboux transformations explicitly.
