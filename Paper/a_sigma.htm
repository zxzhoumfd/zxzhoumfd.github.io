Differential Geometry, World Scientific, Singapore (1993), 325-332

Construction of Explicit Solutions of Modified Principal Chiral Field in 1+2 Dimensions via Darboux Transformations

Zixiang Zhou
Institute of Mathematics, Fudan University, Shanghai 200433, China

Abstract
The Darboux matrices for an integrable modified principal chiral field is constructed, and a proceedure to generate infinite number of explicit solutions is proposed. New solutions are obtained by Darboux transformation with variable spectral parameters.
