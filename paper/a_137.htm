Chinese Physics Letters 21 (2004), 237-238

An observation of relationship between the fine structure constant and the Gibbs phenomenon in Fourier analysis

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract
A value given by a simple mathematical expression is proposed which is close to the fine structure constant given by 1998 CODATA recommended values of the fundamental physical constants up to relative accuracy 10-7. This expression relates closely with the value of the overshoot of the Gibbs phenomenon in Fourier analysis.
