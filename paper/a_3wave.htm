Journal of Mathematical Physics 39 (1998), 986-997

Nonlinear Constraints and Soliton Solutions of 1+2 Dimensional three-wave Equation

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

Using the nonlinear constraint method, the explicit expressions of localized solitons of 1+2 dimensional 3-wave equation are obtained. After l-th Darboux transformation, each component of the solution has at most l2 peaks when the parameters for each single Darboux transformation are large enough. This gives a corresponding asymptotic property for DSI equation, although all the peaks of each component move in the same velocity here.
