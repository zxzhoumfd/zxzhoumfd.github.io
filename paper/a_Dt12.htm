Lett. Math. Phys. 16 (1988), 9-17

On the Darboux transformation for 1+2 dimensional equations

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract
The Darboux transformations in 1+2-dimensional space are construced, with a discussion of their composition and permutability. The auto-Backlund property of the Darboux transformation for the NxN system and KP hierarchy is also proved.
