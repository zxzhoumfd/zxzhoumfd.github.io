Nonlinear Physics, Springer-Verlag (1990), 23-28

Determination of nondegenerate Darboux operators of first order in 1+2 dimensions

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

In 1+2 dimensions, some Darboux operators have been constructed before. In this paper, all the nondegenerate Darboux operators of first order are given for quite general Lax pairs without reduction. They take the form which is already known. The Darboux operators or Darboux matrices in 1+1 dimensions are discussed as special cases.
