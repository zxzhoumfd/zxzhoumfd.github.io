Lett. Math. Phys. 13 (1987) 179-187

On the Darboux matrices of Backlund transformations for the AKNS system

Chaohao Gu

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

The auto-Backlund property of the Darboux matrices for AKNS systems is proved. A quite general form of the theorem of permutability is obtained in a universal way.
