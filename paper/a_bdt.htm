Physics Letters A195 (1994), 339-345

Binary Darboux Transformations for Manakov Triad

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract
The binary Darboux transformations for Manakov triads of second order are constructed, which generalize the work for KP equation and Novikov-Veselov equation. An equation given by [1] is discussed.
