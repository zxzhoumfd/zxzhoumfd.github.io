Physics Letters A211 (1996), 191-198

Binary Darboux Transformations for Manakov Triad (II)

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract
A general formula of the closed 1-form in binary Darboux transformation for Manakov triad of arbitrary order is obtained. The 1-form given by this formula coincides with those for many special PDEs. This is the generalization of the results in the Letter of the same title in Phys. Lett. A195, (1994).
