Separation of variables for the Lax pair of the Bogomolny equation in 2+1 dimensional anti-de Sitter space-time

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

A Lax system separating all the variables is reduced from the Lax pair of the Bogomolny equation for the Yang-Mills-Higgs field in 2+1 dimensional anti-de Sitter space-time. It also contains the Bogomolny equation in 2+1 dimensional Minkowski space-time (Ward equation) under further reduction or limit. The Darboux transformation can be constructed in the usual way to get explicit solutions of the Bogomolny equation.
