Letters in Mathematical Physics 32 (1994), 1-10

On Darboux Transformations for Soliton Equations in High Dimensional Space-time

Chao-hao Gu
Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract
The Darboux transformations for a class of completely integrable systems in the space-time R1+n, which are much more general than the systems in [6], is considered. Similar results are obtained. This class contains the generalization of the Davey-Stewartson equation etc. as special cases.
