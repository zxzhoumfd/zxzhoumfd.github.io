Chinese Physics Letters 22 (2005), 1570-1572

Asymptotic behavior of solitons with a double spectral parameter for the Bogomolny equation in (2+1)-dimensional anti de Sitter space

Xue-Feng Ji and Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

The asymptotic behavior of the solitons with a double spectral parameter for the Bogomolny equation in 2+1 dimensional anti-de Sitter space is obtained. The asymptotic solution have two ridges close to each other which locates beside the geodesic of the Poincare half-plane.
