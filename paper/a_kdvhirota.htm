J. Math. Phys. 54 (2013), 081506

A universal way to determine Hirota's bilinear equation of KdV type

Yi-Chao Ye, Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

A universal way, which is independent of computer programming, is presented to determine whether a given nonlinear partial differential equation can be transformed to a bilinear equation of KdV type via a logarithmic transformation. When it can be transformed, all the parameters are determined so that the bilinear equation is written explicitly.
