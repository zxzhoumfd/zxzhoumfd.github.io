Journal of the Phys. Lett. A168 (1992) 370-374

Explicit solutions of N-wave equation in 1+2 dimensions

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

A linear system in $1+1$ dimensions is found to relate the Lax pair of N-wave equation in 1+2 dimensions. This relation gives a method to obtain explicit solutions of N-wave equation in 1+2 dimensions by Darboux transformation.
