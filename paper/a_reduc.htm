Chinese Ann. Math. 20B (1999), 195-204

Parameters of Darboux Transformation for Reduced AKNS, Kaup-Newell and PCF Systems

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

For the integrable system with u(p,q) reduction, there is a well-known sufficient condition to choose the parameters: the spectral parameters only take two mutually conjugate values and the solutions of the Lax pair should satisfy certain orthogonal relations. In this paper, we prove that, for the AKNS system, the Kaup-Newell system and the principal chiral field (PCF), this condition is also necessary for generic potentials with the u(p,q) reduction. For some other reductions, sufficiency and necessity of more constraints are proved.
