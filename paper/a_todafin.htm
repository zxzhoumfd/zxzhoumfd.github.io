Journal of Geometry and Physics 57 (2007), 1037-1053

Finite dimensional integrable systems related with two dimensional A2n(2), Cl(1) and Dl+1(2) Toda equations

Zi-Xiang Zhou

Abstract

The finite dimensional Hamiltonian systems related with two dimensional A2n(2), Cl(1) and Dl+1(2) Toda equations are obtained and their Liouville integrability is proved. Any solution of these Hamiltonian systems will give a solution of the corresponding two dimensional Toda equations.
